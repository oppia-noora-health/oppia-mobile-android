Collection of your personal information

Our primary goal in accessing, collecting and using your information, is to provide support and guidance in implementing the Care Companion Program in a safe, efficient and smooth manner. More importantly, we only collect and process basic minimal personal information of the user that we consider necessary for the Care Companion mobile usage.

What we collect

Noora Health collects the following types of personal data directly from you or your organisation with respect to the Care Companion Mobile App. This includes:

  • Identity and Profile-related Data: This includes personal information concerning the personal or material circumstances of an identified or identifiable User, e.g. name, photographs, age, gender, area of operation, facility name and facility type where you operate and other similar such details of your area of operation.
  • Contact data: This includes your phone number.

Personal data collected through your use of the Care Companion Mobile App and from your device. This type of data is the personal data and ancillary data required for the purposes of the Care Companion Mobile App usage. It includes:

  • SMS Data: We collect, transmit and store SMS data for the limited purpose of securing the OTP for the signing into the Care Companion Mobile App.
  • Device Data: The information we collect from your device includes IP addresses, SDK version and mobile network information including carrier name, SIM Serial and SIM Slot, your profile information, time zone settings, location and device information for the purpose of onboarding. We collect information about your device to provide automatic updates and additional security so that your account is not used in other people’s devices.
  • Usage Data: We track certain information about you based on your Care Companion Mobile App’s usage. We use this information to do research on our Users' demographics, interests, and behaviours to better understand, protect and serve our Users and improve the functionality and features of the Care Companion Mobile App. This information is compiled and analysed on an aggregated, anonymized basis.

Personal Data Collected from your Organization or our Partner Organizations OR third-parties. This type of data is personal data about you received from your organization. We may also collect data from other organizations who you may have authorised to share such data with us. Please note that we do not have any control over personal data that you may choose to make publicly available. We are not liable for any third-party misuse of such data.

You consent to provide us access to such personal data through your continued use of the Care Companion Mobile App.

What we do not collect

  • Identity or financial information: Details such as your Aadhar card, your bank account, credit/debit cards, Payment Instruments etc,.
  • Health Information: Your Height, Weight, Illness/diseases, Medical Test Reports etc.
  • Other Information: Any Information contained in documents used as proof of Identity and proof of address, proof of age, proof of income.
